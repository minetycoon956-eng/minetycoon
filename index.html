🚀 Getting Started
⛏️ How do I start mining? ⌄

Starting to mine is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Mining tab from the bottom navigation
  2. Click the "Start Mining" button
  3. Choose between:
    • Free Mining - Standard rate (1x)
    • Ad-Boosted Mining - 30% boost for 1 hour
  4. Wait for the 24-hour mining cycle to complete
  5. Click "Mine Again" to start a new cycle

💡 Pro Tip: Use ad-boosted mining during peak hours for maximum efficiency!

💰 How do I earn tokens? ⌄

There are multiple ways to earn tokens:

  • Mining: Automatic every 10 seconds (1 base token + bonuses)
  • Daily Login: 200 tokens every 24 hours
  • Social Tasks: 100-500 tokens per task
  • Watch Ads: Up to 1000 tokens daily
  • Play Games: 50-200 tokens per game
  • Referrals: 10% of friend's earnings

🎯 VIP members earn 20-200% more on all rewards!

⚙️ Mining & Rewards
📈 How is the mining rate calculated? ⌄

Your mining rate = Base Rate × All Bonuses:

  • Base Rate: 1 token every 10 seconds
  • Team Bonus: Up to 2.5x with active members
  • Ad Boost: 1.3x for 1 hour
  • VIP Boost: 1.2x to 3.0x depending on tier

Example: With Gold VIP (2.0x) + Ad Boost (1.3x) = 2.6x rate!
That's 2.6 tokens every 10 seconds instead of 1.

📅 What happens after 24-hour mining? ⌄

When a mining cycle completes:

  1. The timer reaches zero and shows 100% progress
  2. The mining button changes to "Mine Again"
  3. Click it to start a fresh 24-hour cycle

⏰ Your tokens continue accumulating during the cycle!
